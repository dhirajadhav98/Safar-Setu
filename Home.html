Transportation

How to Reach Nashik

By Road

Nashik has excellent road connectivity with major cities. It is easily accessible from Mumbai, Pune, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad, and Bangalore, ensuring smooth travel options.

Mumbai to Nashik: 3.5 hours (₹350-₹600)

Pune to Nashik: 4 hours (₹300-₹500)

Bangalore to Nashik: 14 hours (₹1200-₹1800)

Hyderabad to Nashik: 12 hours (₹1000-₹1500)

Ahmedabad to Nashik: 10 hours (₹600-₹1500)

By Train

Nashik Road Railway Station is a key hub, located 11 kilometers from the city center. It connects to Mumbai, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad and major cities across India including Delhi and Kolkata.

Mumbai to Nashik: 3 hours (₹120-₹500)

Ahmedabad to Nashik: 12 hours (₹300-₹1600)

Hyderabad to Nashik: 14 hours (₹650-₹1500)

Delhi to Nashik: 18 hours (₹800-₹2000)

Kolkata to Nashik: 26 hours (₹700-₹2705)

By Air

Nashik’s nearest airport is Ojhar Nashik International Airport, about 24 kilometers from the city center, offering flights to major Indian cities like Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad And Pune.

Mumbai to Nashik: 45 minutes (₹2500-₹5000)

Hyderabad to Nashik: 1.5 Hours(₹3500-₹7000)

Ahmedabad to Nashik: 2 Hours (₹4000-₹8000)

Delhi to Nashik: Via Mumbai (₹5000-₹8000)

Nearest Int'l Airport: (165 km)

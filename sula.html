About Sula Vineyards

Established in 1999, Sula Vineyards is India's first world-class winery and has pioneered wine tourism in the country. Spread over 3,000 acres in the Nashik region, Sula has transformed this area into India's Wine Capital. The vineyards are located approximately 14 km west of Nashik city on the banks of the Godavari River.

Sula Vineyards offers visitors a complete wine experience with guided tours of the vineyards and production facilities, professional wine tasting sessions, and luxurious accommodations overlooking the vineyards. The winery produces a wide range of wines including red, white, rosé, and sparkling varieties using both international and indigenous grape varieties.

Beyond wine, Sula has become a lifestyle destination with music festivals, restaurants, and adventure activities making it one of the most visited tourist spots in Maharashtra. The property features sustainable practices including solar power, water conservation, and organic farming methods.

Wine Tours & Tasting

Visitors can enjoy guided tours of the winemaking facilities where experts explain the entire process from grape selection to bottling. These tours offer insights into the art and science of winemaking, including visits to the fermentation tanks, barrel rooms, and bottling plant.

The highlight for most visitors is the wine tasting session, where sommeliers guide you through a range of Sula's award-winning wines. The tasting room overlooks the vineyards and offers a perfect setting to appreciate the flavors and aromas of different wine varieties. For enthusiasts, there are premium tasting options including reserve wines and food pairings.

Sula also offers wine workshops where visitors can learn about wine appreciation, food pairings, and even participate in grape stomping during the harvest season (January-March).

Dining & Accommodation

Sula offers multiple dining options including the Italian restaurant Little Italy and the all-day dining venue Soma. These restaurants serve Mediterranean, Indian, and Continental cuisines using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, many from Sula's own organic garden. The outdoor dining areas provide spectacular views of the vineyards and sunset.

For those looking to extend their vineyard experience, Sula provides luxury accommodation options. The Source at Sula is a 23-room resort with modern amenities and vineyard views, while Beyond by Sula offers treehouse accommodations for a unique stay. Both properties feature swimming pools, spa services, and exclusive wine experiences for guests.

Sula Vineyards also hosts the annual SulaFest, a music and wine festival that attracts visitors from across the country. This two-day event features international and Indian artists, wine tasting sessions, gourmet food, and a festive atmosphere celebrating wine culture.
